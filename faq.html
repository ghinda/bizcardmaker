Frequently Asked Questions

How can I edit the details on the business card?
You can edit any of the details on the business card by simply clicking on them, and then editing them as you would using a regular Word processor.
How can I add a logo/picture to the business card?
To add a logo or picture to the business card, click the Upload you logo button, on the business card preview. If you've already uploaded a picture, click it again to upload a different one.
How can I change the text styles (size, weight, font, etc.)?
Select any of the text on the business card, and the text editing toolbar will show up above the selected text, allowing you to edit the text styles.
What's the best logo size?
The width of your logo should be at least 400px, for best print results. The height can be as large or small as you need it, but it's good to have it as to the close to the width value as possible, to keep a square form factor.
What paper should I print the business cards on?
For the best quality use 12pt/350g thick paper.
What is the size of the downloaded PDF?
The PDF you download is 4.45" x 2.95", and contains a single business card with bleed and crop marks to help you cut to the perfect size. It's ready for professional printing, so you can also send it to any printer.
What is the size of the business card?
The business card is 3.75" x 2.25", or roughly 95 x 57 (mm), and includes bleed. Once you've cut the margins from the printed PDF your cards will be 3.5" x 2".
How do you handle the information on the business cards, and elsewhere on the website?
We do not sell, or market, your personal details in any way.
See more details in our Privacy Policy.
